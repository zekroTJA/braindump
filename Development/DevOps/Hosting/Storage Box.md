Storage boxes are fixed amount and price storage solutions by [[Hetzner]]. They are basically stripped down VPS you can store files on and which can be connected via several protocols and applications.

- SSH port 22 (only SCP and SFTP, no interactive access)
- SSH port 23 (interactive access)
- SFTP
- SCP
- FTP
- FTPS
- SMB
- WebDAV

Docs: https://docs.hetzner.com/storage/storage-box/general/
