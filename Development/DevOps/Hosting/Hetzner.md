https://hetzner.com

They are on the more costly edge but they offer very good performing [[VPS]], Storage Solutions ([[S3]] and [[Storage Box]]) and Root Servers. With their [Server Auction](https://www.hetzner.com/sb), you can even get some really strong root servers for very cheap. Also, they have a great [API](https://docs.hetzner.cloud/) and [CLI](https://github.com/hetznercloud/cli) tools available.

Another great thing is that you pay for VPS on an hourly basis. So you can practically spin up an instance just for some quick testing and delete it shortly afterwards without paying a lot for it. Also VPS are very quick up and running.
